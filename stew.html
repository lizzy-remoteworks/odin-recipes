Stew

A good looking and teasty stew

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Fish/egg
  • Groundnut oil
  • Pepper
  • Maggi
  • Onion
  • Meat
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes
  2. Add Groundnut oil and fry
  3. Boil the meat with Spices
  4. Add the tomatoes and onion
  5. Add water and allow to boil
  6. Allow to cook for 20 minutes

Stew is another kind of soup that we eat in Nigeria. You can eat it with rice, yam, potatoes etc

You boil the meat with spices and set aside. Set pot on fire and add oil and onion. Add tomatoes and allow to fry for a while, add more spices.Add the boiled meat and fish. Allow to boil for 20 minutes, serve with rice.
