Moi-Moi

A good looking and teasty moi-moi

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Fish/egg
  • Groundnut oil
  • Pepper
  • Maggi
  • Onion
  • Periwinkle

Steps

  1. Soak the beans for 10 minutes
  2. Peal the cover and wash
  3. Add some ingredients and blend
  4. pour in small plates
  5. Add water to the pot
  6. Set pot with the food on fire
  7. Allow to cook for 25 minutes

Moi-Moi is a very proteinous food that people in Nigeria love a lot.The can eat it with garri or pap, sometmes you can add it to rice aor even eat it alone

Moi-Moi takes a bit of time beause of the process of pealing the beans. Soak beans for soemtimes, wash and remove all the peals. Add ingredients and blend together. Add the blended beans in small plates or cups , set them in a big pot and set on fire
