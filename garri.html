Garri and Egusi Soup

A properly made garri and egusi soup

Ingredients

  • Cassava
  • Egusi
  • Palm oil
  • Meat
  • Fish
  • Onion
  • Periwinkle

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add the garri
  3. Clean the meat and fish
  4. Bring to a boil
  5. Add onion,pepper,maggi
  6. Add oil, crafish,Periwinkle
  7. Allow to cook for 15 minutes

Garri is one of the most popular food in Nigeria. It is made up of just cassava. Allmost every household in this country loves garri.

Garri is easy to prepare. After it has been fried, you simple boil water and add th quantity you want to eat then stir. That is it. you can eat it with any kind of soup such as egusi, okoro,vegetable or ache soup
